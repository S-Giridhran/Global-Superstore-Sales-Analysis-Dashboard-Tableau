# 📊 Global Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard built using the Global Superstore dataset to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and regional business trends. The dashboard provides key business KPIs such as Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, Total Orders, and Average Shipping Days. Users can dynamically filter data by year to monitor business performance and identify growth opportunities across different regions and customer segments.

## 📈 Key Insights
- Total Sales reached ₹26.77 Lakhs with a Total Profit of ₹3.07 Lakhs.
- The overall Profit Margin is 11.48%, indicating healthy business profitability.
- A total of 10,962 orders were processed with an average shipping time of 4 days.
- Central Region generated the highest profit, followed by the North Region.
- Southeast Asia and Canada contributed comparatively lower profits, highlighting areas for improvement.
- Profit margins are distributed almost equally across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office customer segments.
- Current year sales showed an 18.5% growth compared to the previous year.
- The dashboard highlights the Top 10 Revenue-Generating Products, helping identify high-performing products.
- Year-wise sales analysis enables tracking of business growth trends and performance changes over time.
- Interactive year filters allow users to perform dynamic analysis and compare performance across different periods.
